Why Technology


I am a goal driven and compassionate young person with
a love for people and helping others. I used to believe that the best way
to help others was to hold a better understanding of them personally
(this, for me, entailed getting to know them on a deeper and more intimate level)however,
as my life experiences increased so did more innovative and unique ways to help others
started to develop.I began to hold the view that the best way to help others was to
equipt them with the necesseary skills to aid themselves in their struggles and I
believe that is where my love for tech blossomed.I learnt that technology is the best
way to ensure that people are not only equipt with the necessary skills to ensure
their prosperity but it also assists them in a journey of independance, whilst
simultaneously covering a broader spectrum of people. This to me is the best form
of help that can be offered.





What Keeps Me Going?


I am very family oriantated and I cherish my family
dearly. They have truly been my support structure throughout my most toughest times.
I believe that without the family God has blessed me with my world would fail to make
sense, alhough they tend to drive me up the wall at times and test my very limits!
My love for my family does not diminish regardless of these odds though.Through family
I have learnt resilience, pain, love, joy and most importantly a sense of belonging.
It is through these hardships and blatant optimism that I learnt and experienced the
love of God and the power faith in Him truly holds. From as young as I could remeber
my family has been very Christ-centered and I feel as though it has had a major
influence on the way I view life. From morals to principles, I based my standard of
living on this very fiundation handed to me by my parents.God has fulfilled me and
ensured I had hope when life was filled with none.It was within those moments where
I felt God's presence further and was reminded of of His unconditional love.





What Are My Goals and Aspirations?


My goal in life relies upon one thing: To make the most out
of my God given life.In my early years of life my answer would have been completely
different.I would have mentioned something around the love for food and cooking and
perhaps if asked that question back then I would have plainly stated that I would want
to be a chef( even though the concept of cooking itself is quite foreign to me) It is
through these different passions that i came to acknowledge that I cant place myself into
a sngle box. That the answer does not lie within action but purpose and passion, that I
can find happiness in technology, psychology and cooking too! We are complez beings with
complex thoughts and the idea of labelling yourself is limiting. Often when met with
choice of career and passon it is assigned with monetary value and deemed to be "success".
I believe that success is not something that is based soley on the things acquired; that
is the physical side of things but on the experiences found along the way.My goals
therefore is to live life wholeheartedly; allowing love, peace and happiness to take
full effect.
